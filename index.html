Who am I?

My full name is Oscar Lundberg. I'm a frontend developer educated at Jensen Yrkeshögskola.

From an early age I was fascinated with computers. I understood there was a world behind the visible layer, and intrigued to find out more.

In my early twenties I started working on the tech side of online advertising.

The company I worked for had offices around most of Europe and I was responsible for the Swedish branch, tech-wise. My daily tasks included managing ad-campaigns on a network of around 120 websites, manipulating code, making sure they displayed as agreed - and put together reports when the campaign-goals were reached.

Although it was challenging and good work, I was young and felt the need to explore other things in life. I travelled, worked as a freelance musician, got an education in sustainable development, amongst other things.

Now I'm back - I've come full circle, and I'm very happy about it!
